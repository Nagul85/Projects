We are committed to continuing improvement toward an accident-free workplace through effective supervision, education, and training. We expect all supervisors and workers to be dedicated to the continuing objectives of workplace safety and eliminating “near misses”, which will greatly reduce the risk of injuries.

2)   Our philosophy is that the well-being of our company and clients is dependent on the health and safety or our workforce. The Directors and Managers of this corporation promise that every precaution reasonable in all circumstances will be taken for the protection of all workers. Supervisors are responsible to ensure that machinery and equipment required for use by each worker are safe and that each worker works in compliance with established safe work practices and procedures for each piece of equipment. 4)  All supervisors, and subcontractors must protect their own workers’ health and safety and employees are expected to work in compliance with all applicable regulations as well as the safe work practices and procedures established by our company.

5)  We recognize that a safe work environment can be established and sustained only through a united effort by all employees and subcontractors and that the assistance of each person is required. 6)  Everyone from the Directors to new workers has the responsibility to ensure a safe and healthy workplace. Let’s all work together to prevent incidents from creating unwanted losses and personal injuries or illnesses.
