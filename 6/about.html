About us

Welcome To Resource Me India Technologies Private Limited

Resource Me has been incorporated in 1999 with the aim to provide Operation and maintenance services, equipment & spares supplies, Turnkey/EPC solutions, especially in the material handling systems, in Electrical, IT, Steel, Mines, Port & Cement industries.

The company can provide Turnkey Solutions as per the set parameters and standards to ensure the attainment of the specified targets and goals.

Expertise in handling Engineering, Project Management & Procurements including Quality improvement recommendations as well as corrective and preventive actions with diligent planning for On Time completion and deftness in implementing cost reduction measures to achieve savings in the projects.
