Biometric

Biometrics refers to the measurement and analysis of unique physical or behavioral characteristics of an individual. These characteristics may include fingerprints, facial features, iris or retina patterns, voice patterns, or even the way a person walks. Biometric data is collected and stored electronically in order to identify individuals with a high degree of accuracy.

AMC

An Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) is an agreement between a customer and a service provider for the maintenance and repair of a particular product or system. It is a contract that outlines the terms and conditions of the maintenance services to be provided by the service provider, usually on an annual basis.AMCs are commonly used in the electronics and technology industry, where companies offer maintenance and repair services.
